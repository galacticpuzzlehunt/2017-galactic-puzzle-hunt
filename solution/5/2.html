Solution: YOLO Queue

Written by Ben Yang

This puzzle is presented as a series of insulting chat messages delivered by champions in the popular alliterative arena game League of Legends. Each champion has four abilities, which are colloquially called Q, W, E, and R, named for the key you press to activate the ability.

Every chat message references the Q ability of another champion (hinted at by the word “Queue” in the title and the word “cue” in the flavortext), except with one letter changed:

If we pick the correct location to start the cycle, we see a clue phrase in the “After letter” column: SOUND OF SILVER SONG NINE. The 9th song on the album Sound of Silver is NEW YORK, I LOVE YOU BUT YOU’RE BRINGING ME DOWN.

Author's Notes

In League of Legends, there are several ranking tiers, starting at Bronze and Silver and going up to Challenger. The game has a stereotype for having lots of trolls and flamers, so these chat messages can be considered “sounds of silver.”

As a side note, we needed this answer for meta constraints, but the third track on the album would have made a much better final answer: “North American Scum”

Another fun anecdote involves Warwick’s Q. At the end of January 2017, Warwick was redesigned, and his Q was changed from “Hungering Strike” to “Jaws of the Beast”. While proofreading/factchecking the puzzles, our team discovered that while some pages on the official League of Legends website were updated, there still existed some that referenced the old name. As of this hunt's end, there are still some outdated pages on the official website, so apologies to those that might have been confused.
